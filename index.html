Why Use a Local Password Generator?

Unlike other tools, XD Secure Pass generates passwords using your browser's local engine. This means your sensitive data never travels over the internet, protecting you from "man-in-the-middle" attacks.

The Science of Password Entropy

Entropy is a measure of randomness. For maximum security, we recommend a minimum of 60 bits of entropy. Our tool calculates this in real-time using the formula:

$E = L \cdot \log_2(R)$

Where $L$ is password length and $R$ is the size of the character pool.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is XD Secure Pass better than choosing my own password?

Yes. Humans are biased toward patterns (names, dates). Our tool uses window.crypto.getRandomValues, which ensures mathematical randomness that is virtually impossible for a human to replicate.

What is Brute-Force?

Brute-force is a method where hackers use computers to try millions of combinations per second. A 12-character random password would take a standard computer centuries to crack.
